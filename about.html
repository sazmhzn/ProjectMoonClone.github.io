Project Moon is an game studio founded in 2016.
Our base objective is to garner wealth and fame. With those resources,
we hope to engage in various creative activities,
making what we want to make.
Liberating ourselves from the constraints of content...
We wish to tell our stories through diverse mediums,
such as games, comics, novels, and more.
 We hope to continue walking down this path...
With the people who enjoy our stories.
